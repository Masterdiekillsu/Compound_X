
This document records avaialble mechanized augmentations for players. Included are two types: Combat Suits and Combat Frames.

==============================
Combat Frame: An external series of motors and beams designed to augment a user''s strength, speed and or reflexes
==============================

Basic Frame: 20EP
    Armour:
        User''s armour
    Armament:
        1 Arm hardpoint
        Back Slot
    Augmentation:
        Dexterity +1
        Strength +1

Advanced Frame: 25EP
    Armour:
        User''s armour
    Armament:
        2 Arm Hardpoints
        Back Slot
    Augmntation: Tuned to the user''s specifications, pick a Stat and increase by 2, and pick another and increase by 1
------------------------------
Combat Frame Additions
------------------------------



==============================
Combat Suit: A suit that a user dons, fully enclosing the user with protective armour and enhanced mechanical actuation of limbs. While using the Combat Suit and equipped with weapons on one or more hardpoints, the user may only fire one weapon at a time, as if they were using a single weapon under normal circumstances (Unless they have basic, improved or advanced weapon targeting upgrade). If they wish to use a weapon from a hardpoint, there is no Action cost to switch to using another weapon.
==============================

Basic Suit: 30EP
    Armour:
        15 DR
        100%
        4 Primary mags, 2 Secondary Mags.
    Armament:
        1 Arm Hardpoint
        1 Shoulder Hardpoint
        Chest Slot
        Back Slot
    Augmentation:
        Strength +2
        Speed -1m/movement

Heavy Suit: 40EP
    Armour:
        30 DR
        100%
        6 Primary mags, 3 secondary Mags.
    Armament:
        2 Shoulder Hardponts
        2 Arm hardpoints
        Chest Slot
        Back Slot
    Augmentations:
        Strength +2
        Perception +1
        Speed -2m/movement
        
Recon Suit: 65EP
    Armour:
        10 DR
        100%
        4 Primary Mags, 2 Secondary Mags
    Armament:
        1 Back Slot
    Augmentation:
        Dexterity +2 (applies towards increasing movement speed as well)
        25m sensor array
        Darkvision
        Permanent cloaking module with the following stats:
            10 nanites per turn with movement in it. 0 nanites per turn of no movement.
            +15% stealth DC while activated
            +3 turns of cloak per cloak activation.
            minimum recharge rate of 2 turns between cloaks, even at higher levels.
------------------------------
Combat Suit Additions:
All weapons may be equipped with weapon attachments, however the following items do not apply to weapon stats while being used by the suit''s systems: weapon sights, foregrips, slings, bipod, unnderbarrel launcher/shotgun, straightpull bolt.
------------------------------

    Arm Hardpoints:
        Pistol Attachment: Cost and stats of one pistol.
        SMG attachment: Cost and stats of one SMG.
        Rifle Attachment: Cost and stats of one rifle.
        Grapple hook launcher
        Hook (for climbing or fighting)
        Deployable Sword
        Deployable Shield
        Mini Missle
    Shoulder Hardpoints:
        Weapon Attachment Point, Light:
            Pick any Pistol or SMG. Same cost as normal weapon. Reload DC is +30% for awkwardness.
        Grenade Launcher Point: 8EP
            Attachment of a standalone 40mm grenade launcher. See weapon attachments, Lower Rail Attachments, Grenade Launcher for stats.
        Zurlinsk MMG-5 (Mounted Minigun): 14EP
            100m, 50%, 50 rds, 5mm linked. 80 dam (On hit, two hits occur). Reload DC 55%, 2 Full Actions.
    ===============
    Back Slots:
    ===============
        Shield Generator, Class B: 12EP
            Applies a shield with the following stats:
            100%
            100 health
            25 CS per turn of no hit.
        Shield Generator, Class C: 16EP
            Applies a shield with the following stats:
            100%
            150 health
            50 CS per turn of no hit.
        Cloak Generator: 25EP
            Basic Cloaking.
        Improved Cloaking Generator: 35EP
            Stealth DC +20%
            Cloak Duration +1 turn
            Cloak Cost: +5 nanites / turn
        Upgraded Powerplant:
            Allows more useage of suit abilities
    ===============
    Chest Slots:
    ===============
        Shield Generator, Class A: 8EP
            Applies a shield with the following stats:
            100%
            100 Health
            25 CS per turn of no hit. When depleted, 1 recalibration turn is required before recharging can begin.
        Improved Armour: 3EP
            +10 DR
        Advanced Armour: 6EP
            +20 DR
    ===============
    Leg Enhancements:
    ===============
        Enhanced Jumping: 4EP
            Jump 2m vertically, 3m horizontally static.
        Improved Jumping: 7EP
            Jump 4m vertically, 5m horizontally static.
        Enhanced Speed: 3EP
            +1m/movement
        Improved Speed: 5EP
            +2m/movement
        Advanced Speed: 8EP
            +4m/movement
    ===============
    Foot Enhancements:
    ===============
        Jet Boots (aids in jumping)
    ===============
    System Enhancements:
    ===============
        Advanced Waterproofing
        Advanced Movement Management Module (AMMM): 4EP
            Allows use of "speed" and "jump" enhancements simultaneously.
        Autonomous Defensive CrpytoSys: 10EP
            Makes it harder to hack into suit.
            +20% to hacking DC for opponents.
        Increased augmentation: 10EP
            +1 to each augmentation listed.
        Automatic weapon system:
            Spend two Full Actions. for the next X turns, where X = Perception, your shoulder mounted weapon(s) will target and attack a number of opponents equal to your INT / 2 max.
        Add ability:
            Choose an ability from the list.
