These addons are stat affected items or items that will add functionality to a weapon. Most Attachments can be afixed to any weapon unless otherwise noted. Ask a GM if ou are not sure.

====================
Optics / Aiming Aids
You may only have 1 scope, unless otherwise stated.
====================

Tritium Glow Iron Sights 2EP
1x magnification, but allows for much better target aquisition. Can be mounted on 45 degree mounts in addition to a scope in order to negate a minimum engagment distance penalty.
Accuracy -5%
Can be used on any conventional, nonexplosives weapon (E.G. pistols, shotguns, rifles, etc.).

Grenade Launcher Iron Sights 2EP
1x Magnification. Allows better estimation of distances for use with grenade launcher.
Underbarrel Grenade Launcher -10% miss chance.

1x Dot Sight 2EP
Your basic tube-shaped dot-projection. Dot can be changed from blue-green-red for various lighting situations and terrains.
Accuracy -3%
Range +10m

1x Reflex Site 4EP
A reflex-projected site, comprised of a light source and a single pane of rectangular glass. Allows for smaller F.O.V. blockage. Has a small cross, chevron, or dot as the indicator.
Accuracy -5%
Range +10m / +5m for pistol
Can be used on pistols for -1EP

1.2x Hologramic projector 6EP
Projects a hologram target aquisition reticle, which compensates slightly for windage or target movement. also artificially enhances view so as to magnify by 120%.
Range +15m, +5m for pistol
Accuracy -10%
Can be used on pistols for -1EP

nx Magnifier for Dot Scopes n+2EP
A basic magnifying tube capable of increasing the effective range of a weapon. Must be paired with a dot sight.
Range + (n*20)

3.2x Scope 6EP
A heavy-duty tube made for heavy weapons and sustained automatic fire, but would suit single-fire weapons just as well. Sports a black multi-chevron reticle suitable for daytime brightlight use.
Range + 50m, Minimum +10m.

4x Scope 6EP
Range +75m, minimum +15m.
Can only be used on Long Rifles, Carbines.

2-7x Scope 10EP
As a full action, you can adjust the magnification on this scope in order to better see your target. Once sighted to a certain range, other targets that are within 20m can still be fired upon. otherwise you must adjust your magnification.
Accuracy -20%
Range +100m, minimum +10m

7x Scope 8EP
Range +120m, minimum +30m
Can only be used on Long Rifles.

8x Scope 9EP
Range +150m, minimum +35m
Can only be used on Long Rifles.

Range Finder 4EP
Notifies the user of the target's range via an infrared laser beam. Useful for calculated bullet drop for a sniper. When a Sniper takes a full round to adjust it's scope''s windage or elevation due to distance, negate the full round and adjust the scope as a free action.
(can be used in addition to another scope)

IR scope 8EP
Enables visibility in dark settings. This scope does not have any inherent magnification, and needs to be used in conjunction with a scope or dot site.

====================
Assistance Attachments
You may only have one type of laser, and maximum of two total of the following.
====================

Flashlight 2EP
Its a flashlight. It lights things up.
30m visibilty in dark.

IR flashlight 3EP
Works well with IR goggles. Actually, it only works with IR goggles.
30m visibility in dark with IR goggles.

Bipod 3EP
Requires one full action to deploy the bipod. user can diengage for a free action. After disengaging, user must still take a full action to deploy. Cannot be used with any foregrip or underbarrel weapon.
Accuracy -20%

Red Laser 3EP
Accuracy -10% when Range < 50m

Green Laser 3EP
Accuracy -10% when Range > 60m

IR Laser 5EP
Accuracy -10% when range <50m. Must be used with IR goggles or an IR sight.
Can also be used to paint a target for Airstrikes or Orbital Strikes.

====================
Barrel Attachments
You may only choose one barrel type, and one barrel attachment.
====================

Muzzle Break 2EP
Reduces Recoil from the gun, allowing for more accurate follow-up shots.
Accuracy -5%

Flash / Sound Suppressor $100
Greatly reduces the muzzle flash and some of the weapon report from firing. Targets are not likely to see you or hear you when not particularly looking for you. When alerted to your presence, they are 60% less likely to find your position.
Range - 10m
Not useable on shotguns or HMGs

Improved Flash / Sound Suppressor $300
Completely removes muzzle flash and the weapon report. Targets cannot locate you due to firing your weapon.
Range -20m
Damage -5
Not useable on Shotguns or HMGs

Light Profile Barrel 3EP
When a conventional ammunition weapon requires a STR check to carry or use, subtract 1 from the STR check.
Hit DC +10%

Heavy Profile Barrel 3EP
This heavier barrel reduces recoil, increases the accuracy of follow-up shots.
Accuracy -10%, STR +1

===================
Slings
===================

Pistol Lanyard 2EP
If you drop your pistol, it''ll fall harmlessly to your leg level instead of dropping onto the ground.
Holstering your weapon is a free action. Drawing your pistol takes a full action next time.

Two Point Sling 3EP
You can use your sling to stabilize your grip arm. -5% accuracy.
Reduce strength needed by 1.

One Point Sling 4EP
Due to the positioning of the weapon, you can draw your primary as a free action.
Reduce strength required by 1. Holstering your weapon is a free action.

Ammunition / Magazine Accessories
===================
Extended Magazines 3EP
Adds +2 rounds to Long Rifles and Shotguns, +5 rounds to Pistols and Carbines, +10 rounds to Assault Rifles & SMGs.

Hollow Point Magazine 2EP
Increases damage to targets +15/-15 for unarmoured/armoured

Armour Piercing Magazine 2EP
Increases damage to targets +15/-15 for armoured/unarmoured

CS tipped Magazine 2EP
Inflicts a CS burn on hit, dealing d10/2 damage for 2 turns, +5 damage agsint robots.

RMM Match Grade Ammo 2EP
This high quality ammunition isn''t cheap, but it increases the consistancy of your shots. -10% miss chance.

High Velocity Ammunition 5EP
Increased velocity grants a +10% increase to range, and +10 damage.
(Available for all standard munition guns)

Shotgun Slugs	0EP
Instead of firing a number of pellets and a wad, a slug is a single large 
projectile.
Increase miss chance by 15%. Increase range by 20m. Reduce net damage by 2 
pellets and deal that damage in a single projectile (so that armor damage
mitigation only affects hits once).

Magpulls 3EP (2EP for pistols)
Decrease the Reload DC by 5% for any box magazines. (Applicable to Pistols, SMGS, Some Shotgunns, Carbines and Assault Rifles)

====================
Total Conversions
You may only utilize one weapon conversion at a time. HOWEVER, for conversions relying on switching out a reciever, you may carry at max one additional receiver in order to swap kits on the go. You must pass 2 Weapons use / knowledge [Weapons] checks in order to successfully convert your weapon on the go. DC 60. Failure on one check instantly jams the weapon on first shot. Failure on both checks requires an additional check.
====================

Revolver Bottom-Cylinder Fire 5EP
Modifies the pistol to fire from the bottom cylinder rather than the top, decreasing the kick from firing.
Accuracy -15%

Revolver Gas Routing System 6EP
Adds a gas tube or piston system which automatically cocks the hammer when a round is fired. Turns any revolver into a semi-automatic revolver.

Pistol Hot-Load Reinforced Slide 3EP
This heavier slide allows the pistol to fire Magnum loads, increasing damage output of the pistol. Not useable with revolvers or plasma pistols.
+10 Dam

Pistol Lightened Machining slide 8EP
This lighter weight slide utilizes carbon fiber reinforced polymer with a metal shell to provide a lightweight slide that retains strength for full-auto and burst fire.
See SMG rules for firing. Not useable with Revolvers.

Pistol - Carbine kit 8EP
A tactical frame bolts around the shooter''s pistol, and a longer barrel is attached. Accuracy at longer range is now attainable, along with the ability to mount up to 4x magnification, foregrips, etc. Modified Pistol is still considered a CQB Weapon.
+50m range

Sawn-off shotgun barrel 3EP
Range - 15m

Plasma Generation Chamber 20EP
Turns your boring, standard munition guns into a god-killing, blue plasma belching death machine. Weapon keeps its base range and accuracy. Increase damage by 25 and add plasma burn. Increase strength requirement by 1. Your weapon now uses appropriate plasma packs as ammunition. Pistols = 8 rds, shotguns = 7rds, SMGs = 30rds, Carbines = 20rds, Assault Rifles = 25, Long rifles = 10rds.

Straight Pull for ECSC PSR, Anvil Arms SR-250 5EP
Allows the user a Straight-pull action to chamber a new round, increasing rate of fire. With DEX higher than 7, you can fire 1 round every Action.

Standalone Grenade Launcher 2EP
A polymer and metal frame to mount an underbarrel grenade launcher. Allows use of an underbarrel grenade launcher as a secondary weapon, freeing up the underside of a primary for other attachments.
