Name: Monk John Frank
Description of Attitude, demenor, etc:
Monk Frank has left his montessori in order to travel through the galaxy, searching for those who would abuse technology and set them on the right Path. He tries to be calm and think through situations, often asking himself what his mentors would do or say. He maintains neutrality in most disputes, seeking to avoid violence whenever possible. This does not however mean he is timid in any way, and he will often take initiative when necesary.

Physical Description:
5'2 and balding, Monk Frank is aged 38. He has been training for most of his life and remains extremely fit and mobile. He has deep blue eyes and a well kempt shotish beard.
