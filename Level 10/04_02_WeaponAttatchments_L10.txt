Ammunition / Magazine Accessories
Total mag price should be calculated by taking multipliers for extended mags 
first, then multipliers for special effect, then magazine accessories like 
magpulls.
===================
Drum Mag $2.5x magazine cost.
Increases the capacity of your rifle by a substantial amount.
250% increase. Takes up twice the space in your magazine inventory as a
standard magazine. Only applicable to box-fed automatic rifles [not belt-fed], 
SMGs, Carbines, and shotguns.


Phase Rounds $4x mag cost
RP Effects. Also -20% miss chance.

Vorpel Rounds $25*mag price
	On a roll of a natural 96-100, instead of dealing extra-crit damage, the 
	shooter scores a headshot, removing the head of the target. If the target 
	would normally die from losing its head, it does so.
